### Rules

**I â€“ All electronic devices (phones, tablets, laptops, etc.) to be switched off and handed over upon arrival**\
Most importantly, this rule would give everyone a rare chance to unplug from their daily commitments and social addictions. **II â€“ Morning rituals, breakfast, and beach walk in silence**\

**III â€“ Lunch and dinner to be had together with one conversation at the table**\
