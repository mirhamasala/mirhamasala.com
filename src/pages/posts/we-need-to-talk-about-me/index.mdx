## 🐣 Standards I Hold Myself To

### Mastered

- Apologize properly and as soon as possible _(related reading: [Apologies](/letters/paper-planes-packets-0220/))_
- Elaborate compliments and give credit where credit is due
- Be on time
- Don’t use the catchphrase _I’m busy _(recommended reading: [The Busy Trap](https://opinionator.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/06/30/the-busy-trap/) by Tim Kreider)\_
- Don’t cancel meetings last-minute (works well when only setting meetings max. a week out)
- If they were agreed first, honor dates with friends and family above impromptu work requests
- No gossiping, bad-mouthing
- Refrain from asking for silly favors (Recommended forwarding: [Let Me Google That For You](https://letmegooglethat.com/))

### Work in progress

- “If you have a choice between being right and being kind, choose kind.” –Dr. Wayne W. Dyer

- Make the charitable assumption

  - “Making the charitable assumption means that when someone behaves poorly, you should avoid judging them and instead give them the benefit of the doubt.” –[The Culture Code](https://www.amazon.com/Culture-Code-Secrets-Highly-Successful-ebook/dp/B019CGXU68)
  - Recommended listening/reading: [This is Water](https://fs.blog/2012/04/david-foster-wallace-this-is-water/) by David Foster Wallace

- No complaints

- Sleep on it
